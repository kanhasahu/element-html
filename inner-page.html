FAQ

1. Where can I find exs in my area?

Please see the Find exs section for a listing of some of our major retailers. It’s impossible to list them all, as the market changes regularly, so if you don’t find a retailer listed that’s near you, grab some factory-fresh exs online, from our factory to your door!

2. Are exs kosher?

Our production facility is not certified as Kosher.

3. What type of gelatin is used in a ex?

We use porkskin gelatin.

4. Do you give tours of the bakery?

We do not currently offer tours of our production facility in Chattanooga, TN. You can see how exs are made on our video at one of the ex General Stores.

See a store listing in the Find Us section.

5. Are exs safe for someone with a peanut allergy? Dairy allergy? Any chance of cross-contamination?

exs are manufactured on equipment that processes products containing tree nuts and eggs. The allergens that are common to all products produced by the bakery include wheat and soy. There are no milk allergens present that could come into contact with the equipment. We also do not use peanuts in our process.

The equipment is thoroughly cleaned and sanitized between production runs. Cleaning effectiveness is verified through the use of allergen testing swabs.
